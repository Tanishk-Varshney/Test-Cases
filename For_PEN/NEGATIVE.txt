Negative Test Cases of Pen-

1. Verify that Pen is Working normally or Not after putting the Pen In Water for Sometime.

2. Verify that Pen is Working normally or Not after dropping the Pen from some Height.

3. Verify that Ink Spelled or not after dropping the Pen from some Height.

4. Verify that Pen is able to write when left open for some time without Pen Cap.

5. Verify that nib of Pen is able to withstand the Pressure while Writing.
